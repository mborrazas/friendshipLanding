Questions? Answers.

A Friendship Lamp is ideal for anyone looking for a unique way to stay in touch with their loved ones. Whether you have family spread across the world, are currently in a long-distance relationship, or have a special friend you'd like to stay in touch with, our Friendship Lamps are specially made for you. In fact, it's even a great piece to place in your office and connect with your family and friends at any time of the day. Regardless of your location or theirs, a Friendship Lamp is the best way to send your love across miles.

You can use the lamp however you like! But remember: every time you touch it, all other lamps connected to the same group ID will light up.

Yes, the other lamp or lamps light up as soon as you touch your lamp. There is no delay unless there is an issue with the Wi-Fi connection at either end.

Friendship lamps are fun to use with one person or in a small, medium, or large group! Just make sure all lamps in that group use the same group ID. Once entered into each lamp, you'll be able to send your fun greeting to your entire group.

Friendship lamps have unlimited colors, as you can choose exactly what color you want using our app. By using the app, you can choose any shade of color you desire, so you're not limited at all by your color choice.

Electrical connection
Our Friendship Lamps are connected via USB, so all you need is an exclusive power adapter with a USB port and you'll be ready to go. We don't provide a power adapter, so you can use the same type of adapter you would use for your phone charger. All you need to do is plug in the USB cable that comes in the box with the Friendship Lamp. WiFi requirements
Lamps need to be connected to WiFi to function. Currently, they are only compatible with 2.4GHz WiFi. This is the current standard and has better range. However, most newer routers of 5 GHz also have an option for 2.4 GHz WiFi. If there is no WiFi connection available, you can also connect the lamp to a mobile hotspot or public WiFi (such as university WiFi).
App installation
Friendship Lamps require a unique setup, and you'll need to install the HuugLight app on your iOS or Android phone to connect it to your WiFi.
